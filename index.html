Good question.

  1. The conversations are meant to be more immediate than email, but not as personal as text.
  2. Email doesn't as easily organize all CougarTech conversation in one place.
  3. Everyone can be a (polite) part of the conversation.
  4. Since mentors have access to all the channels, there is oversight and supervision to help everyone stay on point.
  5. Not everyone has easy access to their email outside of school.
  6. Not everyone has access to texting, and texting requires lots of separate groups.
